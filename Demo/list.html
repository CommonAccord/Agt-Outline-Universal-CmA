As examples of contracts made from the "universal" outline, these two NDAs show a Mutual NDA between two companies and a One-Way NDA between a company (as Disclosing Party) and an individual (as Receiving Party). They use the same outline structure and library of materials. The layers, are:
  1. The specific terms - parties, dates, etc; on
  2. a shape for Entities or Humans; on
  3. a shape for Mutual or One-Way; on
  4. a library for NDA; on
  5. the Universal Outline.
